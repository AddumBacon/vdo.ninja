# \&nowebsite

Sender-Side Option! ([`&push`](../../source-settings/push.md))

## Aliases

* `&noiframes`
* `&noiframe`
* `&nif`

## Details

Disables IFRAMEs from loading, such as remotely shared websites by another guest or director.
