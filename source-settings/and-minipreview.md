# \&minipreview

Sender-Side Option! ([`&push`](push.md))

## Aliases

* `&mini`

## Details

Shows the mini self-preview at the top right corner. This is the default behavior when [`&broadcast`](../advanced-settings/view-parameters/broadcast.md) mode is on.
